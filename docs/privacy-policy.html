🔱 Disha — Privacy Policy

ଦିଶା ଆପ୍ — ଗୋପନୀୟତା ନୀତି

Effective Date: April 19, 2026

Disha (ଦିଶା) is a bilingual Odia-English daily companion app for the people of Odisha. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

ଦିଶା ଆପ୍ ଓଡ଼ିଶାର ଲୋକଙ୍କ ପାଇଁ ତିଆରି। ଏହି ନୀତି ବ୍ୟାଖ୍ୟା କରେ ଯେ ଆମେ କେଉଁ ତଥ୍ୟ ସଂଗ୍ରହ କରୁ ଓ କିପରି ବ୍ୟବହାର କରୁ।

1. Information We Collect · ଆମେ ଯାହା ସଂଗ୍ରହ କରୁ

Data Why Stored Where
District selection To show local weather and mandi prices for your area On your device (local storage)
Push notification token To send festival reminders, holiday alerts, and weather warnings Supabase (our secure database)
Family messages To enable messaging between family members Supabase (our secure database)
App preferences
(language, font size, notification settings)		 To remember your settings On your device (local storage)

2. Information We Do NOT Collect · ଆମେ ଯାହା ସଂଗ୍ରହ କରୁ ନାହିଁ

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Precise GPS location (only district is selected manually by you)
  • Payment or financial information
  • Contacts, camera, microphone, or files
  • Browsing history or data from other apps

3. Third-Party Services · ତୃତୀୟ ପକ୍ଷ ସେବା

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Supabase Database for messages and push tokens supabase.com/privacy
Open-Meteo Weather data (no personal data sent) open-meteo.com/terms
Expo (by Expo Inc.) Push notification delivery expo.dev/privacy

4. How We Use Your Data · ତଥ୍ୟ ବ୍ୟବହାର

  • To show you relevant local weather, mandi prices, and festivals for your district
  • To deliver family messages between linked accounts
  • To send you optional push notifications (festival reminders, weather alerts)
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or third parties

5. Data Security · ତଥ୍ୟ ସୁରକ୍ଷା

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption (TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest). Family messages are private and only accessible to linked family members.

6. Data Retention · ତଥ୍ୟ ସଂରକ୍ଷଣ

Messages are retained until deleted by the user. Push tokens are removed when you disable notifications. Device preferences are stored locally and deleted when you uninstall the app.

7. Children's Privacy · ଶିଶୁ ଗୋପନୀୟତା

Disha is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights · ଆପଣଙ୍କ ଅଧିକାର

  • You can disable notifications at any time in Settings
  • You can change or clear your district selection at any time
  • To request deletion of your data, contact us at the email below

9. Changes to This Policy · ନୀତି ପରିବର୍ତ୍ତନ

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
